About Me

Over the past eight years I worked as an educator. During that time I developed a passion for creating things that help people learn in a fun, interactive, and meaningful way. I enjoy working with collaborative, passionate people while remaining determined to be achievement-oriented and flexible.

As a self-motivated, lifelong learner I enjoy synthesizing information I have researched and analyzed to create products for other people. This lead to my interest in learning web development and attending a programming bootcamp so I could create things that delight and inspire people to learn while making their lives more enjoyable.

I decided to pursue a career in web development because I enjoy creating, learning, and being challenged intellectually. In my spare time you'll find me enjoying any kind of dessert, especially chocolate, or baking some sweets at home.
